Kitfo

Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish made from finely minced raw beef mixed with spiced clarified butter (niter kibbeh) and a special chili spice blend called mitmita. It is often served with injera, and sometimes lightly cooked or served completely raw (known as leb-leb or tire).

Ingredients

  • Raw minced beef
  • Spiced butter
  • Mitmita (chili spice)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Melt niter kibbeh in a pan (optional light cooking).
  2. Mix in mitmita and salt.
  3. Add finely minced raw beef.
  4. Stir gently until well coated (or lightly cook if preferred).
  5. Serve immediately with sides.
