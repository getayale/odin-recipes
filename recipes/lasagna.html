lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide flat pasta sheets with ingredients like minced meat, tomato sauce, cheese (especially mozzarella), and sometimes vegetables. It is then baked until hot and golden.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef with onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato sauce and seasonings, simmer.
  3. Boil or prepare lasagna pasta sheets (if needed).
  4. Layer pasta, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers until full.
  6. Bake until golden and bubbling.
