Beyaynetu

Beyaynetu is a traditional Ethiopian vegetarian platter made up of different small portions of foods served together on a single plate, usually placed on top of injera.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Cabbage
  • Potatoes
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cook each vegetable/lentil separately.
  2. Season each with spices, salt, and oil.
  3. Prepare different small dishes (lentils, cabbage, potatoes, etc.).
  4. Arrange everything on injera in separate portions.
  5. Serve as a mixed platter.
